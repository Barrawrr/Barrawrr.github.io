About the Project

This project was part of our Design Production class, where we were assigned to work directly with small local businesses in need of branding support ideally those without any visual identity yet.

My team collaborated with a home-based laundry soap business that already had a basic brand but needed a redesign for their X-Banner and product listing. I helped plan the banner layout strategy to ensure it would stand out effectively in its placement area..

Separately, I handled the design of their product catalog entirely on my own. From layout structure to visual hierarchy and content organization, I made sure it was both practical for customers and aligned with a more polished brand presentation.
