Students ID Card SMKN 59 Jakarta

A collaborative project with the school team to create digital student ID cards that are not just functional, but also clean and visually polished. I was in charge of visual editing to ensure each card had a consistent and professional look.

About the Project

This project was a formal assignment from school, where I collaborated with two teammates to design and produce Student ID Cards for the 26th cohort of SMKN 59 Jakarta.
My main responsibility was managing student data. I coordinated with each class representative to ensure that all personal information was collected accurately and completely.

I was also responsible for editing the student photos. My main focus was adjusting the lighting and contrast to make sure the background didnâ€™t blend too much with the subject. I used Adobe Photoshop for the editing, and each final photo was resized to fit the standard 2x3 ID photo format ready to print with no extra steps needed.

Beyond just the technical aspect, this project gave me hands-on experience in data management, inter-class communication, and teamwork all of which helped me better understand how to handle real-world production workflows for visual identity projects.
